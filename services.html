Engagement patterns

Timelines vary by service and data readiness. Below are common engagement shapes we use to keep scope clear and delivery predictable.

Rapid workflow fixes

Best for: MX Deposit setup, exports, schema cleanup, small automations.

  • Discovery: quick review of current setup + goals (days)
  • Delivery: implementation + handover (days to ~2 weeks)
  • Outputs: working workflow, documentation, training
Field-season support

Best for: QA/QC monitoring, geochem rollups, campaign consistency.

  • Kickoff: define checks, thresholds, reporting cadence
  • Ongoing: weekly/biweekly reviews + updates
  • Outputs: dashboards/reports, decisions documented
Targeting / prospectivity builds

Best for: prospectivity maps, integrated targeting, uncertainty + explainability.

  • Discovery: objectives, deposit model, data audit (1–2 weeks)
  • Pilot: baseline model + validation (2–6 weeks)
  • Scale: iterations, new data, target ranking
Systems & governance

Best for: databases, validation rules, pipelines, reporting standards.

  • Discovery: architecture + data reality (1–2 weeks)
  • Build: staged implementation (weeks)
  • Handover: documentation + team training
