Representative engagements

These are typical engagement shapes and deliverables. Specific client names and project details are confidential.

Exploration data system setup

Typical deliverables: governed schema, validation rules, reproducible exports, and a handover package.

  • Database or platform configuration (including MX Deposit workflows)
  • QA/QC checks (interval overlaps, required fields, sensible ranges)
  • Repeatable exports for geologists, GIS, and modeling
QA/QC program and batch review

Typical deliverables: control charts, thresholds, exception logs, and decision-ready summaries.

  • Standards / blanks / duplicates review
  • Bias, drift, and out-of-control detection
  • Clear recommendations for follow-up and re-assay where needed
Geochemistry anomaly screening

Typical deliverables: anomaly maps, ranked targets/intervals, and campaign-consistent comparisons.

  • Multi-element anomaly detection with domain filters
  • Separation of analytical artefacts vs signal (QA gating)
  • Follow-up tables designed for field planning
Prospectivity and target ranking

Typical deliverables: prospectivity surfaces, feature rationale, and a short list with uncertainty context.

  • Mineral systems framing + ML (when appropriate)
  • Explainability (feature contribution / sensitivity)
  • Target ranking to support decision-making
Text & knowledge mining for desktop studies

Typical deliverables: searchable summaries, source-linked extracts, and thematic syntheses.

  • Assessment + literature consolidation
  • Structured extraction (geology, structure, age, mineralization)
  • Faster synthesis for due diligence and targeting
Bespoke tools & automation

Typical deliverables: scripts/apps, documentation, and training so your team can run it.

  • Custom pipelines and repeatable reporting
  • Dashboards or lightweight apps
  • Prototype-to-production support
