About SMCG

SMCG is a geoscience consulting group co-led by professionals with decades of combined experience in exploration and applied data science. We build ML-ready data systems, prospectivity and anomaly-detection workflows, and decision support that scale from greenfields to advanced projects. Our work is confidential by default; public materials describe capabilities and outcomes, not proprietary methods or datasets.

Confidentiality. We do not list clients or project specifics publicly. Detailed case studies and proposal microsites are available by invitation under password protection.

Services

Exploration Data Systems

End-to-end stewardship: design, automation, and governance (not day-to-day DB admin)

  • Architecture & standardization of drill, geochem, geophysics, mapping
  • Migration planning & scoped imports (MX Deposit expertise)
  • Validation rules & QA/QC pipelines embedded in workflows
  • Automated reports & dashboards for decision-makers
  • Reusable automation to reduce manual effort
Prospectivity & Targeting

Hybrid ML + Mineral Systems to rank targets with explainability

  • Feature engineering from structure, physics, geochem, proximity
  • Modeling with transparent validation & feature importance
  • Scope-dependent target ranking, uncertainty bands, drill-ready shortlists
Anomaly Detection & Trend Analysis

Find meaningful geochemical/geophysical anomalies and patterns — cleanly and reproducibly

  • Outlier & cluster detection across multi-element datasets
  • Campaign-aware comparisons to confirm persistence
  • QA checks to separate signal from noise and lab/processing artefacts
  • Maps & interval summaries highlighting follow-up areas
Text & Knowledge Mining

Turn large volumes of reports and literature into usable insight

  • Consolidate historical reports, memos, assessments, and open sources
  • Extract key facts: geology, structures, ages, mineralization, locations
  • Surface relationships & themes to inform targeting and due diligence
  • Deliver searchable summaries, source links, executive-level briefs
Bespoke Solutions

When standard tools don’t fit, we build tailored workflows.

  • Custom data pipelines & automation
  • Experimental ML/AI workflows for unique datasets
  • Prototype dashboards & decision-support apps
  • Collaborative R&D at reduced rates when outcomes benefit both sides
