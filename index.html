About SMCG

SMCG is a geoscience consulting group co-led by a team with decades of combined experience in exploration and applied data science. Our current offerings focus on building ML-ready data systems, prospectivity workflows, and decision-support tools that scale from early-stage greenfields to advanced projects. As the industry evolves, so will our services—helping explorers adapt to new technologies and opportunities.

Services

Exploration Data Systems

End-to-end data stewardship: design, automation, and governance (not day-to-day DB admin)

  • Architecture & standardization of drill, geochem, geophysics, mapping
  • Migration planning & scoped imports (MX Deposit expertise)
  • Validation rules & QA/QC pipelines built into workflows
  • Automated reports & dashboard visualizations for decision-makers
  • Reusable automation (exports, checks, notifications) to reduce manual effort

Note: We scope migrations carefully to avoid runaway effort; focus is on robust design & automation so your team scales smoothly.

Prospectivity & Targeting

Hybrid ML + Mineral Systems to rank targets with explainability

  • Feature engineering from structure, physics, geochem, proximity
  • Modeling with validation & feature importance for transparency
  • Scope dependant target ranking, uncertainty bands, and drill-ready shortlists
Anomaly Detection & Trend Analysis

Find meaningful geochemical/geophysical anomalies and patterns—cleanly and reproducibly

  • Outlier & cluster detection across multi-element datasets
  • Time-/campaign-aware comparisons to confirm persistence
  • QA checks to separate signal from noise and lab/processing artefacts
  • Clear maps & interval summaries highlighting follow-up areas
Text & Knowledge Mining

Turn large volumes of reports and literature into usable insight

  • Consolidate historical reports, memos, assessments, and open sources
  • Extract key facts: geology, structures, ages, mineralization, and locations
  • Surface relationships & themes to inform targeting and due diligence
  • Deliver searchable summaries, source links, and executive-level briefs
Bespoke Solutions

Exploration is full of one-off problems that data can help solve. We build tailored workflows when standard tools don’t fit.

  • Custom data pipelines & automation
  • Experimental ML/AI workflows for unique datasets
  • Prototype dashboards & decision-support apps
  • Collaborative R&D at reduced rates when outcomes benefit both sides

Ideal for early-stage, high-uncertainty challenges where innovation is part of the value.
